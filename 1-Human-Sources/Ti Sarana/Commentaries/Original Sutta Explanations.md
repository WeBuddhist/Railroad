01st SOURSE from Mahānāma Sutta - Aṅguttaranikāya

"Yato kho, mahānāma, buddhaṃ saraṇaṃ gato hoti, dhammaṃ saraṇaṃ gato hoti, saṅghaṃ saraṇaṃ gato hoti; ettāvatā kho, mahānāma, upāsako hotī"ti.

Tipiṭaka (Mūla)\ Suttapiṭaka\ Aṅguttaranikāya\ Aṭṭhakādinipātapāḷi\ 3. Gahapativaggo\ 5. Mahānāmasuttaṃ\ 
https://tipitaka.theravada.su/canon/sutta/an/8/25/p/38760

English Translation by Bhikkhu Bodhi - 
"When, Mahanama, one has gone for refuge to the Bud­dha, the Dhamma, and the Sahgha, in that way one is a lay follower."

02nd SOURSE from Dakkhiṇāvibhaṅga Sutta - Majjhimanikāya

Yaṃ hānanda, puggalo puggalaṃ āgamma buddhaṃ saraṇaṃ gato hoti, dhammaṃ saraṇaṃ gato hoti, saṅghaṃ saraṇaṃ gato hoti, imassānanda, puggalassa iminā puggalena na suppatikāraṃ vadāmi, yadidaṃ – abhivādana-paccuṭṭhāna-añjalikamma sāmīcikammacīvarapiṇḍapātasenāsanagilā- nappaccayabhesajjaparikkhārānuppadānena.
