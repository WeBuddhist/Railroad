## Term analysis

### `བཅོམ་ལྡན་འདས་མ་ (bcom ldan 'das ma)`

Commentary 1 presents a variant 'འཕགས་པ་' (phags pa, noble/arya) instead of 'བཅོམ་ལྡན་འདས་མ་' (bcom ldan 'das ma, Blessed Mother/Bhagavati). This would be a meaning-altering change from a specific epithet for a female Buddha/deity to a more general term. However, Commentaries 5 and 7, along with the explicit Sanskrit mapping in Commentary 7 ('བྷ་ག་ཝ་ཏི་ནི་བཅོམ་ལྡན་འདས་མ།'), confirm 'བཅོམ་ལྡན་འདས་མ་' as the authoritative reading, directly translating Sanskrit Bhagavatī. Therefore, the source text's reading is correct and no standardization is needed.
