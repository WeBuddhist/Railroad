## Gloss Divergences

⚑ **འཕགས་པ་**: The source text and Commentary 7 omit 'འཕགས་པ་' ('phags pa, Noble One/Arya) from the title, while Commentary 1 includes it. The Sanskrit source 'Ārya-bhagavatī-prajñāpāramitā-hṛdaya' confirms the inclusion of 'Ārya', hence it is added in the standardized text.

## Standardized text

བོད་སྐད་དུ། འཕགས་པ་བཅོམ་ལྡན་འདས་མ་ཤེས་རབ་ཀྱི་ཕ་རོལ་ཏུ་ཕྱིན་པའི་སྙིང་པོ།

A significant semantic variant was corrected by adding 'འཕགས་པ་' ('phags pa, Noble One/Arya) before 'བཅོམ་ལྡན་འདས་མ་' (Bhagavatī) in the title. This correction is based on the authoritative Sanskrit title 'Ārya-bhagavatī-prajñāpāramitā-hṛdaya', where 'Ārya' is present, and is supported by Commentary 1 which includes 'འཕགས་པ་' in its title. The omission of 'འཕགས་པ་' in the source text constitutes a meaning-altering variant as it removes a key epithet from the full designation of the text.

## Term analysis

### `བཅོམ་ལྡན་འདས་མ་ (bcom ldan 'das ma)` (discrepancy)

The source text omits the term 'འཕགས་པ་' ('phags pa, Noble One/Arya) before 'བཅོམ་ལྡན་འདས་མ་' (Bhagavatī). This omission is a significant semantic variant because 'འཕགས་པ་' is a standard epithet in the full Sanskrit title 'Ārya-bhagavatī-prajñāpāramitā-hṛdaya', where 'Ārya' directly corresponds to 'འཕགས་པ་'. Commentary 1 also includes 'འཕགས་པ་' in its title, supporting its inclusion. Its absence alters the complete designation of the text.
