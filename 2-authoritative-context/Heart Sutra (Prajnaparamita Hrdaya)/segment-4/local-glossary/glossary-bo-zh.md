No meaning-altering variants or significant expansions beyond standard prose explanations were found.

### `དགེ་སློང་གི་དགེ་འདུན་ཆེན་པོ་དང་།`

Commentaries (Commentary 1, 2, 3, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, 10) consistently interpret 'བྱང་ཆུབ་སེམས་དཔའི་དགེ་འདུན་ཆེན་པོ་དང་ཐབས་ཅིག་ཏུ་བཞུགས་ཏེ།' (byang chub sems dpa'i dge 'dun chen po dang thabs cig tu bzhugs te) as 'together with a great assembly of bodhisattvas, dwelled'. No meaning-altering variants were found. The phrase 'དང་ཐབས་ཅིག་ཏུ་' (dang thabs cig tu) is a standard way to express 'together with', and 'བཞུགས་ཏེ་' (bzhugs te) consistently means 'dwelled' or 'resided'.
