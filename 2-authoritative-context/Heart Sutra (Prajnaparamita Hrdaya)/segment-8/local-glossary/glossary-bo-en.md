## Term analysis

### `ཟབ་མོའི་ (zab mo'i)`

The source text includes 'ཟབ་མོའི་' (zab mo'i, profound's), which is crucial for specifying the nature of the practice. Commentary 18 omits 'ཟབ་མོའི་', which would alter the meaning from 'profound practice of the Perfection of Wisdom' to simply 'practice of the Perfection of Wisdom'. However, since the source text retains 'ཟབ་མོའི་', and it is supported by the majority of other commentaries (1, 2, 5, 8, 12, 13, 16) and the UCCA interpretation, no correction is needed for the source text itself. The omission in Commentary 18 is a meaning-altering variant, but the source text is already authoritative.
