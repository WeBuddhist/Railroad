## Term analysis

### `ཟབ་མོའི་སྤྱོད་པ་ (zab mo'i spyod pa)`

The source text uses 'ཟབ་མོའི་སྤྱོད་པ་' (zab mo'i spyod pa), including the genitive particle 'འི་'. Some commentaries (e.g., Commentary 8, 10, 12) present 'ཟབ་མོ་སྤྱོད་པ་' (zab mo spyod pa), omitting the genitive. This omission is a common grammatical or metrical variation in Tibetan texts and does not alter the fundamental meaning of 'profound practice'. The UCCA semantic interpretation also aligns with the source text's inclusion of 'འི་'. Therefore, this is not considered a meaning-altering textual variant.
